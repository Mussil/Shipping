Introduction

This simulation introduces an innovative delivery system based on the crowds which are already on the road.
Parcels can be delivered by more than one driver so that drivers collect parcels from stations around the city and drop them off at other stations towards the destination.

In this simulation you can control the paramaters of the amount of drivers for the next two days, parcels and customer preferences.
During the simulation run you will be able to view the parcels transferred and get the information about them.
