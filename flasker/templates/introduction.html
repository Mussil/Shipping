Introduction**

This simulation introduces an innovative delivery system based on the crowds which are already on the road, so they collect packages from stations around the city and drop them off at other stations towards the destination.
In this simulation, it is possible to control the number of drivers participating in the system as well as the number of packages that are expected to be sent and possible customer preferences, thus giving it:
1. of packages are shipped
2.number of packages were not shipped
3.cost
4.number of drivers
This simulation was created as part of a research project in software engineering where algo develops an innovative delivery system based on packages being delivered by more than one driver, as well as the masses - the drivers who travel on the roads anyway at another station that brings the package closer to its destination.
In the following pages you can control the parameters that determine the number of drivers who will participate in the trip for the next 2 coming days as well as the number of packages that are expected to be sent.
You can then view the package routes transmitted by the drivers according to the above algorithm and get the data describing:
the amount of packages that managed / failed to reach their destination
the number of drivers who participated in the transfer of each and every package
estimated cost to the customer for a package depending on the route
the length of time it takes for a package to reach its destination
the distance a package travels depending on the route
